Frequently Asked Questions

How can I change my reservation's pick up location or vehicle?

To change your pick up location or vehicle, you must cancel your current reservation and start a new one.

When will my card be charged for my reservation?

If you choose to pay online, your card will be charged when you pick your vehicle up.

Will I be charged a late fee if I bring my car back after the deadline?

You will not be charged a late fee for returning your vehicle past your drop off day. However, you will be charged for any extra days you keep your vehicle.

Can I return my vehicle while the store is closed?

Yes, you may drop your vehicle off using our convenient key dropboxes. You will not be charged an additional fee.

How old do I have to be to rent a vehicle?

You must be at least 18 years old to rent a vehicle from Speedcats.

My question wasn't answered here. How can I contact you?

Use our contact form to send us any questions not answered here.
