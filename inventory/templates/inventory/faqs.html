Frequently Asked Questions

How can I change my reservation's pick up location or vehicle?

To change your pick up location or vehicle, you must cancel your current reservation and start a new one.

When will my card be charged for my reservation?

If you choose to pay online, your card will be charged when you pick your vehicle up.
